Better with Kids

Toledo's top kid-friendly outings

Toledo's top kid-friendly outings.

Glass City Metropark
Toledo's new, world-class, riverfront park. Something for kids of all ages. A massive climbing slide and playground for bigger kids, a smaller scale playground for younger kids, and a waterpark perfect for the little ones. Skate all winter long at the Ribbon and then bring your scooters and roller skates in the warmer months. Sit back at the restaurant/cafe and enjoy the views.
Main Library - Children's Area
A wonderful space in a beautiful building. Let your kid wander or check out some of the terrific programming.
Toledo Museum of Art - Children's Area
Another Toledo institution with an inviting children's area. An endless supply of crafts and projects for your kids.
Toledo Zoo
One of the top ranked zoos in the nation for a reason!
Imagination Station
Great year round, but an absolute parent favorite when you need an active indoor option for your kiddos.
Local Sports
All affordable, entertaining, and kid friendly. Check out our guide to local sports for more info.
Ottawa Park Playground
Brand new accessible playground. Turn your kids loose here for hours on end.
