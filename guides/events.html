Better Events

The Goldilocks zone for events

Toledo is in the Goldilocks zone for events. Big enough to support numerous festivals and entertainment acts, while small and weird enough for the random and unexpected.

To illustrate: pick a random September weekend, focus only on Downtown, and limit yourself to a $0 budget. You'll still have more options than you can fit in.

A Sample Weekend

Friday Night

Strongman Champions League
Outside Middlegrounds Metropark, 4-7pm. Random, weird, entertaining, and free.

Saturday

Downtown Farmers' Market
8am to 1pm. The best food in Toledo from your neighbors. Or just wander around if you're serious about the $0 budget.
PARK(ing) Day
Toledo's spin on the global movement to transform parking spaces into public spaces. Huron Street, 11am-5pm.
Artomatic 419
Local art festival at the Erie Street Market, 2-10pm. Exhibits by community artists, live music, and films.

Sunday

Garmin Marathon
Watch the runners wind through Downtown. Cheer for strangers.
Artomatic 419 (Day 2)
11am to 3pm. Head back for what you missed.
Oktoberfest at Maumee Bay Brewing
1-8pm. Music, beer, and games.
