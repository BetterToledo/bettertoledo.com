Better Food

A guide to eating better in Toledo

Toledo is more than chicken wings, burgers, pizza, and chains.

1 Home Cooking

The Farmers' Market
Load up on top notch produce, meats, dairy, bread, and more from your neighbors at a fair price. Don't trust us? Compare a tomato here with one from Kroger.
→ Transform grocery shopping from a chore into a weekly highlight
Phoenix Earth Food Co-Op
Your spot for bulk staples.
Share your cooking with friends
The best meals are the ones you share.

2 Learn How to Cook Deliciously

Not into cooking yet? Attend cooking classes for a great meal and a good time.

Cork & Knife
Arguably the best food in Toledo. Check out their pop-up events too!
Sofia Quintero Art & Cultural Center
Cooking classes with cultural depth and community.

3 Visit St. Kengo Street

Join our efforts to rename St. Clair Street in honor of the beloved family of Kengo restaurants:

Kengo
World-class sushi.
Kato
Delicious ramen.
Papa K's
Best fried chicken in town.

Toledo's wheelhouse is Lebanese food. Try these first:

Souk
Terrific food with big city vibes.
The Beirut
A Toledo institution.
Middle East Market
Stop by the deli for our favorite hummus in the city.
