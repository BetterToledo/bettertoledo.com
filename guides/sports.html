Better Sports

Majoring in the minors

Toledo is spoiled with affordable high-level sporting events. Whether you want a good time and could care less about the outcome, or you're a die-hard looking for a community of true fans, Toledo has options for you.

The Teams

Toledo Walleye
The best value for your sporting entertainment dollar in North America. $20 gets you in the door for a genuinely electric atmosphere great for families, finatics, and casuals alike. Think we're overstating things? Go to a playoff game and see for yourself.
Toledo Mud Hens
Minor league baseball at its best in the heart of Downtown. A family favorite that doubles as Toledo's favorite beer garden.
UT Women's Basketball
Unbelievable fan base and support for one of the best programs in the country. An absolute gem that embraces the community beautifully.
UT Men's Basketball
Same great arena as the women, same standard of excellence.
UT Football
Historic venue on a beautiful campus. NFL talent and tons of wins.

Moneyball Value

What's the theme here? High end entertainment and performance for incredibly accessible prices.

For the price of one Red Wings, Tigers, or OSU/Michigan game you can go to 5 games in Toledo. Get in and out in minutes without the headaches.
